Our Story

Here at Garden State Barbershop we are family friendly establishment and value every client.

We take passion in giving high quality detailed men’s grooming services.

Your image is everything and we uphold the highest standards in our industry.

We strive to be a barbershop of strength and support to our community.

Experienced Barbers With a Passion For Style & New Ideas

Our passion for cutting hair is the heart and soul of our barbershop. With every snip and shave, we pour our dedication into crafting not just haircuts, but experiences that leave our clients feeling confident, refreshed, and ready to take on the world. Barbering isn't just a job; it's an art form, and we relish the opportunity to express our creativity through each unique hairstyle.

It's a privilege to see the smiles on our clients' faces as they leave our shop, knowing that we've played a part in making them look and feel their best. Our barbershop is more than a business; it's a haven for grooming and camaraderie, where our shared passion for the craft brings us and our clients together.
